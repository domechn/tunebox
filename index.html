TuneBox

A vintage-styled desktop music player for YouTube Music, featuring a retro 1960s-70s aesthetic.

Download Now View on GitHub

Beautifully Retro

Wood grain textures, brass accents, and an amber-glow display.

Features

YouTube Music

Full playback control through YouTube Music's web interface seamlessly integrated.

Vintage UI

Wood grain texture, brass knobs, and an amber screen with scanline effects.

Synced Lyrics

Displays the current lyric line in real time as your music plays.

Rotary Volume Knob

Drag-to-rotate volume control with haptic-style feedback for a tactile feel.

Live Track Info

Real-time song title, artist, and album art display directly on the vintage screen.

Session Persistence

Remembers your login state and volume across restarts automatically.
